How to Use the To-Do App

  1. Enter the Title of your task in the Title field.
  2. Enter a brief Description of the task.
  3. Click the Submit button to save your task.

Delete a Task

To remove a task, click the Delete button next to it in the list.

Update a Task

  1. Click the Update button next to the task you want to modify.
  2. Edit the Title or Description as needed.
  3. Click Update to save your changes.
